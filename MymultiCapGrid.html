A Grid of My Capabilities

My professional journey in B2B Telecom/ICT has equipped me with a versatile skill set, allowing me to excel in nine distinct, yet interconnected, functional roles. These roles capture my multi-dimensional approach to connecting technology, strategy, and commercialization to drive growth and innovation.

1. Product Portfolio Architect

Designs, structures, and manages ICT/fixed product portfolios (cloud, data center, cyber, IoT, connectivity) to ensure lifecycle alignment and profitability.

2. Go-to-Market Strategist & Executor

Converts product concepts into market traction through GTM campaigns, positioning, and adoption strategies.

3. Strategic Alliance Builder

Creates partnerships, alliances, and M&A strategies to expand capabilities and access global ecosystems.

4. Connectivity Monetizer

Transforms telco infrastructure (fiber, subsea cables, POPs, colocation) into commercialized B2B and wholesale services.

5. Value Proposition Creator

Crafts customer-centric propositions that translate complex ICT into tangible business benefits.

6. Commercial & Pricing Modeler

Creates competitive pricing, packaging, and ROI-driven business models to maximize profitability.

7. Sales Enablement Catalyst

Equips sales teams with tools, narratives, and incentives to win in enterprise ICT markets.

8. ICT Marketer

Markets ICT solutions (cloud, managed services, cyber, IoT) to reposition telcos as enterprise solution providers.

9. Telco-to-TechCo Transformer

Reshapes legacy telcos into ICT-driven TechCos by introducing new verticals, revenue streams, and digital ecosystems.
